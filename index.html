Dr. Amit began her professional education at the Kibbutzim College of Education in Tel Aviv, where she studied for a teaching diploma in the unique educational program “Critical Pedagogy, Social Justice and Education for Peace”. She continued her studies at the Tel Aviv University with a B.A in Hebrew Literature. Dr. Amit later obtained a Master’s degree at the Tel Aviv University in the program for Gender Studies. She holds a Ph.D. in Israeli Studies from SOAS, University of London. She is the author of two books: "Moving On From Bliss" (Tel Aviv: Am-Oved, 2016) & "A Queer Way Out: The Politics of Queer Emigration from Israel" (SUNY, 2018). She taught Hebrew in various locations to students from various backgrounds and of various ages.
